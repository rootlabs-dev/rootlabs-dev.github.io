About Heavenly Peace Memorial Park

Dedicated to preserving memories in a tranquil, natural setting.

Heavenly Peace Memorial Park is a family-owned sanctuary nestled in the lush landscapes of Negros Occidental. We believe that every life deserves a beautiful and peaceful final resting place.

Our dedicated team provides compassionate guidance, helping families choose the perfect memorial lot or plan ahead. We are committed to maintaining the park's natural beauty and providing a place where families can find comfort for generations.

Our Mission: To offer a sacred space where love and memories live forever.
