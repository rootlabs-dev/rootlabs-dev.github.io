About Sukro Hotel

A modern hotel in the heart of Bacolod City, built on the values of comfort, convenience, and warm hospitality.

Who We Are

A Modern Hotel Rooted in Bacolod

Sukro Hotel is a contemporary hospitality destination in Bacolod City, the capital of Negros Occidental — known as the "City of Smiles." Designed for today's traveler, our hotel blends sleek modern architecture with the warmth and friendliness the Philippines is famous for.

Operated by Isecure Realty Corporation, Sukro Hotel is a proud addition to Bacolod's growing accommodation scene. Whether you're visiting for business meetings, family events, the famous MassKara Festival, or a quiet weekend getaway, our team is dedicated to making your stay comfortable, convenient, and memorable.

Our property features thoughtfully designed rooms, modern amenities, and a prime location on La Salle Avenue — putting you within easy reach of the city's main commercial, dining, and cultural destinations.

Our Mission

To deliver consistently clean, comfortable, and value-driven accommodations — paired with warm Filipino hospitality that makes every guest feel at home.

Our Vision

To be Bacolod's most trusted address for modern, mid-range accommodation — known for service excellence and a genuinely personal touch.

Our Values

Comfort, cleanliness, courtesy, and consistency. We hold ourselves to high standards in everything — from the sheets on the bed to the smile at check-in.
