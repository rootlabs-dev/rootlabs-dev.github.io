About Miliview

Rooted in Bacolod, Built for Rest

Set along Lopez Jaena Street in Barangay 31, Miliview Residences is a small boutique stay designed around comfort rather than spectacle — clean, quiet rooms, soft lighting, and the kind of service that remembers your name.

We're close to Bacolod's everyday rhythms — markets, cafés, transport — while staying a calm step removed from the noise. Whether you're here for a weekend or a few weeks, Miliview is built to feel like home.

"A place to put your bags down, breathe, and actually feel like you've arrived."
