Our Story

Bacolod Hospitality, Reimagined

Miliview Residences sits along Lopez Jaena Street in Barangay 31 — close to everything that makes Bacolod the "City of Smiles." We built this place for guests who want hotel-level service with the relaxed feel of staying somewhere familiar.

From quick weekend getaways to longer work stays, our team keeps things simple, friendly and genuinely Bacolodnon.
