About Us

A Residence at Heart, A Hotel in Spirit

Tucked along Lopez Jaena Street in Barangay 31, Miliview Residences brings together the privacy and comfort of a home with the warm, attentive service of a boutique hotel. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or an extended stay, our rooms are designed to feel less like a stopover and more like your own place in Bacolod.

We're proudly Bacolodnon — minutes from the city's best dining, markets and landmarks, and just a short trip from the famous MassKara energy that gives this city its nickname: the City of Smiles.

  • Prime location — walking distance to local eateries, shops and transport links.
  • Homey interiors — every room is furnished for comfort, not just a night's sleep.
  • Personal service — a small team that genuinely knows its guests.
