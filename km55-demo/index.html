Our Story

About the Villa

A Place Built for Memories

Vacation Villa KM55 is a family-owned compound nestled in the mountains of Brgy. Bunga, Salvador Benedicto, Negros Occidental — about 55 kilometers from Bacolod City.

Named after its kilometer marker, the villa was built with one vision: to give families, friends, and couples a place where they can truly rest, reconnect, and celebrate life's best moments.

Surrounded by tall trees and the cool highland air of Negros, our compound blends modern comfort with natural warmth — from pinewood-lined rooms to sparkling pools and lush garden pathways.
